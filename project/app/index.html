Pacific Dataviz · 2001–2026

From Track to Toll

Does stronger wind really explain who suffers most?

Using Pacific cyclone records from 2001–2026, this story tests how far lifetime peak wind aligns with the reported share of people affected. Here, “toll” means reported people affected—not a death count. Follow the path from physical storm tracks to country-level impact records across Pacific islands.
